Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our mobile dental services

What insurance do you accept?

We are an out-of-network provider, which means we can work with most insurance plans. We'll provide you with the necessary documentation to submit claims to your insurance company for reimbursement. We also offer affordable self-pay options.

How often do you visit facilities?

We regularly visit facilities every three months for routine care. Our clinical team works with your facility to select the best dates for visits by our hygienists and dentists.

What equipment do you bring?

We bring portable equipment directly to your patients, including a portable digital x-ray system, a complete portable operatory system, and all instruments needed for comprehensive dental services. It's all self-contained and designed for on-site care.

Do you treat bedbound residents?

Yes, we have special equipment and training to provide dental care to bedbound residents directly in their rooms when necessary.

How do you handle emergencies?

We offer priority scheduling for dental emergencies and can often accommodate urgent needs within 24-48 hours. We also provide guidance for managing dental emergencies between visits.

What areas do you serve?

We provide onsite dentistry throughout Utah, including Salt Lake City, Sandy, South Jordan, Provo, Lehi, Layton, Ogden, St George, and Bountiful. Contact us to confirm if we service your specific area.
