Career Opportunities

Join our team of dental professionals making a difference

Work with Dental On Location

Are you a dental professional passionate about making a difference in seniors' lives? Dental On Location offers unique career opportunities in mobile dentistry throughout Utah.

We're always looking for compassionate, skilled dentists and dental hygienists who share our commitment to bringing quality dental care directly to assisted living residents.

Why Join Our Team?

Meaningful Work

Make a real difference in the lives of seniors who might otherwise have limited access to dental care.

Flexible Schedule

Enjoy a schedule that allows for work-life balance with planned facility visits throughout Utah.

Supportive Team

Work alongside experienced professionals who are passionate about geriatric dental care.

Variety of Settings

Experience different facilities and communities as you bring dental care across Utah.

Professional Growth

Develop specialized skills in mobile dentistry and geriatric dental care.

Patient Relationships

Build lasting relationships with residents through regular quarterly visits.

Positions We're Looking For

We regularly seek qualified dental professionals

Dentists

Licensed dentists with experience or interest in geriatric dentistry and mobile dental care.

  • Valid Utah dental license
  • Compassionate patient care
  • Interest in senior dental health
  • Flexible scheduling availability

Dental Hygienists

Licensed hygienists who excel at patient communication and gentle cleaning techniques.

  • Valid Utah hygienist license
  • Gentle cleaning techniques
  • Excellent communication skills
  • Experience with seniors preferred
