Our Services

Comprehensive dental care delivered directly to your facility

Full Range of Dental Services

Professional, highly-experienced dentists and hygienists provide the latest in technology with a full range of procedures - all brought directly to your assisted living facility.

Dental Exams

Thorough oral health assessments including visual examination, oral cancer screening, and comprehensive treatment planning tailored to each patient's needs.

  • Comprehensive oral evaluation
  • Oral cancer screening
  • Personalized treatment plans
  • Digital record keeping

Digital X-Rays

Portable digital radiography equipment for accurate diagnosis with minimal radiation exposure. Instant results allow for immediate treatment planning.

  • Portable digital x-ray system
  • Minimal radiation exposure
  • Instant digital results
  • Secure digital storage

Professional Cleanings

Gentle yet thorough dental cleanings to remove plaque, tartar, and stains. Our hygienists use comfortable techniques specifically designed for senior patients.

  • Gentle cleaning techniques
  • Plaque and tartar removal
  • Fluoride treatments
  • Oral hygiene education

Fillings

Same-day cavity repair using modern filling materials. We provide comfortable, efficient treatment to restore tooth function and prevent further decay.

  • Same-day treatment
  • Tooth-colored options
  • Pain management
  • Long-lasting results

Crowns

Crown repairs and replacements to restore damaged teeth. We work with quality dental labs to ensure proper fit and function.

  • Crown repairs
  • New crown fittings
  • Quality materials
  • Proper fit guaranteed

Root Canals

Root canal therapy performed on-site to save infected teeth and relieve pain. Our experienced dentists ensure comfortable treatment.

  • Pain relief treatment
  • Tooth preservation
  • Infection removal
  • Experienced practitioners

Extractions

Safe, gentle tooth extractions when necessary. We prioritize patient comfort and provide clear post-procedure care instructions.

  • Gentle extraction techniques
  • Pain management
  • Post-procedure care
  • Follow-up monitoring

Teeth Whitening

Professional whitening services for a brighter, more confident smile. We offer safe whitening options appropriate for seniors.

  • Professional-grade whitening
  • Safe for sensitive teeth
  • Noticeable results
  • Maintenance guidance
